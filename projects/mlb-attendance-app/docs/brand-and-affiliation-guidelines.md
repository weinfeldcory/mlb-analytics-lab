# Witnessed Brand And Affiliation Guidelines

Last updated: 2026-05-06

## Current Working Brand

The product brand is `Witnessed`.

## Positioning

Witnessed is a baseball-first live-event history product. It helps fans track the games they attended, preserve memories, and discover what they witnessed.

Current MVP scope:
- MLB/baseball game tracking
- personal stats and memory capture
- private beta social/following layer around baseball history

Future-facing expansion language:
- broader sports and live-event support is planned later
- do not describe concerts or multi-sport support as live unless the product actually ships it

## Approved Taglines

- `Your live-event history, starting with baseball.`
- `Track what you witnessed live.`
- `Remember every game, concert, and moment you saw live.`

Use the third option only in future-facing brand docs or concept work. Do not use it in current MVP user-facing copy without clarifying that the product is still baseball-first today.

## Non-Affiliation Copy

Preferred long-form copy:

`Witnessed is an independent fan project and is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by Major League Baseball, MLB Advanced Media, or any MLB club.`

## Copy Rules

- Use `Witnessed` as the visible product name.
- Keep baseball-first framing in active app copy.
- Prefer `history`, `record`, and `what you witnessed` over generic `attendance app` language.
- Do not imply official league, team, or venue affiliation.
