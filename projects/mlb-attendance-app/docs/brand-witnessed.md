# Witnessed

Last updated: 2026-05-06

## Product Name

`Witnessed`

## Positioning

Witnessed is your live-event history, starting with baseball.

It helps fans track the games they attended, preserve the memories around those nights, and discover what they witnessed over time.

## Current MVP Scope

- MLB/baseball game logging
- personal baseball stats and milestones
- memory capture for attended games
- friend discovery and following around baseball history

## Future Expansion Language

Approved:
- `Broader sports and live-event support is planned later.`
- `Witnessed starts with baseball and expands over time.`

Avoid:
- `Track every sport and concert now`
- `All live events are supported`

## Approved Taglines

- `Your live-event history, starting with baseball.`
- `Track the games you attended and discover what you witnessed.`
- `Track what you witnessed live.`

## Approved Short Descriptions

- `Witnessed is a baseball-first live-event history app.`
- `Track the games you attended, preserve memories, and discover what you witnessed.`
- `Build a baseball history you will actually want to revisit.`

## Words To Avoid

- `MLB Attendance App` as the product name
- `attendance app` as the primary brand description
- `concert tracker` or `multi-sport platform` in current MVP copy
- any language implying official MLB affiliation

## Non-Affiliation Copy

`Witnessed is an independent fan project and is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by Major League Baseball, MLB Advanced Media, or any MLB club.`
